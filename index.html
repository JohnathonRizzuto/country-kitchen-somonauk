About the Kitchen

Real food, honest hours.

Country Kitchen has been Somonauk's morning meeting spot for as long as anyone remembers. Eggs scrambled on a flat-top, hash browns crispy at the edges, biscuits made from scratch. Lunch is comfort food the same way: pot roast, meatloaf, club sandwiches stacked tall.

We open at 5 AM and close at 2 PM — if you need a real breakfast before work, we're already going.
